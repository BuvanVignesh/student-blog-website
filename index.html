Places You Must Visit

prithvi cafe

i had visited back in dec 2018

Finally after pondering over when to visit this cafe, I ultimately managed to make it to this cafe yesterday evening. Though I reached at 7 pm, the cafe was crowded and people were awaiting their turns. My visit to this cafe was actually basis the reviews and the photos that I had seen on Zomato and this exactly matched my expectations. This cafe is at a walking distance from the Tulip Star hotel. This is in the same complex as the Prithvi Theatre. An open air cafe. As we visited in the evening it was fully lit up with bulbs. Its nicely surrounded by trees and has a seating area for around 50-60 pax. As you enter the cafe, there is also a bookstore. If you wanna notice actors from theatre or serials this is one of the place where they can be seen. Coming to the stuff we ordered: Had ordered for couple of pav bhajis and suleimani chai. Would rate the pav bhaji among the best that I would have had anywhere. Coming to the service....when we ordered for the pav bhaji, the waiter told us that it would take 20 mins for it to be served, but actually got it in 10 mins.

jantar mantar

i had visited back in jan 2379

I normally don't like being negative but this "attraction" should be closed until it is refurbished. The Jantar Mantar in Jaipur is far and above this iteration in New Delhi. If you are into astronomy go to the one in Jaipur. I am a history buff but there are no signs or explanation for anything. The price of admission for foreigners is ridiculous for what you get. This appears to be just an area for young local couples to meet each other. This is simply not worth your time. Sorry
